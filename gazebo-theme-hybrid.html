Gazebo theme — hybrid approach

Blue for data values. Teal for navigation (tabs, active states). Links are black with bold weight, turning blue on hover. This keeps blue meaningful (it always means "data") while teal signals "where you are." Links don't compete with either — they're neutral until interacted with.
